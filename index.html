Health Code Violations
of Boston's Food Establishments (2012-13)

Made by Bryce Lambert with data from Boston Open Government

This map plots the health inspection failures of Boston's restaurants--a data set available here. Unfortunately, like a whole lot of textual data sets out there, it doesn't come very clean. It's generously peppered with inconsistent formatting, data entry, spacing, capitalization, and abbreviations. These take considerable ironing out. More importantly, chunks of data important to this map--like location--are missing. The point of all this being that this map is neither the fairest nor the most accurate representation of the state of Boston's restaurants.
